# 🎾 Padel Sweetspot Selector

Find your ideal padel racket sweetspot in seconds — based on how you actually play.

## 🚀 What it does
This tool helps you understand which racket shape fits your game best:

- Round → Control & forgiveness  
- Teardrop → Balanced performance  
- Diamond → Power & aggressive play  

Instead of guessing specs, it focuses on the one thing that really matters:
👉 **Sweetspot position**

---

## 🧠 Why it matters

Most players don’t struggle because of technique.

They struggle because:
- the sweetspot is too high  
- too small  
- or simply not where they expect it  

This tool fixes that.

---

## ⚙️ How it works

Answer 3 questions:
- Shot consistency  
- Playstyle  
- Court position  

→ Get an instant recommendation + visual explanation
